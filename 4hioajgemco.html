August 21, 2019

I did a very short bike workout this morning. It's amazing that it had a perceptible effect on my mood. Less anxious, happier, music sounds better. There's an interesting balance then: if I don't train, I feel badly; if I over-train, I feel badly; if I train just right, I feel great. That seems reasonable enough. Hard in practice though.
Anyway, back to my morning workout.
To lose 15 lbs, I'll need to burn 52,500 calories more than I consume. This morning I burned 214 calories. This feels like the beginning of a long journey.

September 4, 2019

I've come back from vacations in pretty terrible condition! I decided to wrap up my paddling for the season as I'm pretty sure after nearly two weeks off, I'd be starting fresh from nothing and back to tipping into the now-cold water. I don't have the gear for cold-weather paddling. So that's it for now! Time to look ahead to next season. Now's my chance to put in a good base and make some big fitness improvements ahead of next season. The goal is to start next canoeing season a lean, fit 175 lbs with good overall and core strength and stability. What I learned this year is that canoeing really, really rewards doing it a lot. If I paddle 5-6 times a week next summer, I'll be in pretty good form. Maybe even do some racing. CANMAS is definitely my goal. Maybe not next year.
So what are my plans? What's my training plan for the Fall/Winter? I'm definitely going to do strength and erg training at the club. That's the only way for me to do heavy lifting and exercises like bench pull. It will also keep me connected to the club. That's not enough though. Twice a week is not going to be quite enough for me to achieve my goals. 
First, cycling: cycling is the best way for me to get fit and to lose weight. I have so much experience, I have the right setup and I have a decent base. I've been training fairly regularly since last December and that's a pretty substantial amount of work to draw upon for future training. I've committed to an ambitious 37 week sprint training plan.
