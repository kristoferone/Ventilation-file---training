August 21, 2019

I did a very short bike workout this morning. It's amazing that it had a perceptible effect on my mood. Less anxious, happier, music sounds better. There's an interesting balance then: if I don't train, I feel badly; if I over-train, I feel badly; if I train just right, I feel great. That seems reasonable enough. Hard in practice though.
Anyway, back to my morning workout.
To lose 15 lbs, I'll need to burn 52,500 calories more than I consume. This morning I burned 214 calories. This feels like the beginning of a long journey.
