When I do weight training, I'll take exactly the same approach that I always do: sweet spot training. That is to find the optimal amount of work I can repeat continuously for a given amount of time. For example if I'm going to do 20 minutes with 1 min recovery between sets, I need to find an amount of weight and number of repetitions that make that possible.
